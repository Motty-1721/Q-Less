ABOUT Q-LESS

Your ultimate source for live music experiences, exclusive artist interviews, and the latest in concert culture.

Our Mission

Q-LESS Live World Productions, Inc. is dedicated to creating unforgettable live events that connect fans with their favorite artists. We specialize in producing concerts, festivals, and music showcases that highlight both local talent and international stars.

Our Background

Founded with a passion for music and community, Q-LESS has grown into a leading live events company in the Philippines. Our commitment to quality and innovation has made us a trusted name in the industry, known for delivering memorable experiences to music lovers nationwide.

Over the years, Q-LESS has partnered with renowned artists and brands, producing events that span genres and styles. Our team's expertise and dedication ensure every show is a unique celebration of music and culture.

Our Vision

As we continue to evolve, Q-LESS remains focused on supporting emerging artists and fostering a vibrant music community. We believe in the power of live music to inspire, unite, and create lasting memories.

Beyond events, Q-LESS also operates a record label and recording studio, providing artists with the resources and platform to share their music with the world. Our dedication to the craft and culture of music drives everything we do.
